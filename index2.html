The Impermanence Recital

Poster for Chris Uzzel's junior recital. Chris Uzzel is a heavy metal musician. In the past, when I have made things for Chris, he enjoyed the barren look of trees in the winter. Early one morning, I took a picture of freshly-fallen snow which I used for the background.

Later, I printed the posters and gave them to Chris to put around SUNY Purchase's campus. I also gave him a .gif version of the poster to put on Facebook.
