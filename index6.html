Hudson Valley MOCA Moving Logo

While I worked as an intern at Hudson Valley MOCA, (formerly HVCCA) I created a moving version of their logo. Then, I used footage from the setup of a banner in front of the museum and faded that footage into the logo. This was posted to social media to promote the rebranding of the museum and the grand opening of their new exhibit: Death is Irrelevant.
