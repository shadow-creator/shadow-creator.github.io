Museum of Natural History Rebranding

Concept work for logo, business cards, and letterhead for the American Museum of Natural History.

For this project, I started with drawings of shells, pyramids, and bones. However, I realized that the museum was not just these things, but many. So, I decided to try something which showed the essence of the museum. I tried to make the logo blocky so it looks similar to a monument.
