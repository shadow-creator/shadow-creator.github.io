the re.membering Poster

Poster for a multi-media opera inspired by the poetry of Nayyirah Waheed.

In the poem, Waheed says "you must write yourself," so I thought a pencil would be appropriate imagery. In her poems, she always puts her title at the bottom of the poem, so I tried to do the same while still keeping the correct type hierarchy.
