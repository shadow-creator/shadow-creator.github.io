Pillars Vodka

This work is a concept for Pillars Vodka which is made in Sonoma County. The idea is that it would mix an old-fashioned feeling with modern design.

At first, I found it difficult to figure out how to make a label in a unique way. However, I came up with the idea of the label twisting all around the bottle.
